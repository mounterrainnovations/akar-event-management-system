New Genre x Ravensbourne University: Hot Stuff!

Turning up the heat at Ravensbourne University London 🌡️ New Genre partners Mihai Toma and Leon Brown recently joined the Graphic Design students at Ravensbourne for a portfolio review session. The Theme? Hot Stuff. Their most radical work — the unapologetic, concept-pushing kind. This batch of emerging designers came through. From bold typography to sizzling visual identities, to cultural street campaign or immersive installations in TouchDesigner, we left feeling inspired by the warmth & energy of this cohort. The future of the industry is looking flaming-bright.
