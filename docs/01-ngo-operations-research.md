## 1. What Do NGOs Like Akar Women Group Do?

### 1.1 Core Mission Areas

An EdTech + Women & Child Welfare NGO like "Akar Women Group" typically operates across **three intersecting pillars**:

| Pillar                 | Activities                                                                                               |
| ---------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Education & EdTech** | Digital literacy, vocational training, AI/tech workshops, online courses, skill certifications           |
| **Women Empowerment**  | Self-Help Groups (SHGs), entrepreneurship programs, financial literacy, legal aid, livelihood generation |
| **Child Welfare**      | Child education sponsorship, nutrition programs, after-school programs, health & hygiene awareness       |

## 3. Key Metrics NGOs Track

- **Total beneficiaries reached** (per event, per quarter, per year)
- **Funds raised** vs. target
- **Volunteer hours contributed**
- **Sponsorship revenue**
- **Event attendance rate** (registered vs. showed up)
- **Training completion rates** (for EdTech programs)
- **Skill certification counts**
- **Media impressions** and social reach
- **Repeat donor retention rate**
- **Cost per beneficiary impact**
